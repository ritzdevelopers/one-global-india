About One Global

One Global is a next-generation real estate developer focused on building premium, design-led residential destinations that align with global expectations. Our approach combines thoughtful planning, strong delivery partners, and a sharp understanding of what modern luxury truly means, privacy, space, discretion, and long-term value.

As India's luxury real estate market evolves, One Global is creating a platform where branded collaborations, credible execution, and refined living experiences come together in a way that feels timeless, not trend-driven.

Mission

Create high-quality residential environments that elevate everyday living through thoughtful design, credible partnerships, and disciplined execution.

Vision

Build a globally respected real estate platform from India, delivering landmark addresses across select Indian and international markets with integrity and restraint.
